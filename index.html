Hello! I'm Payton Jewell and I'm a front-end web developer.

Meet the Developer

I am a 22 year old front-end developer based in Wisconsin. I entered the web development world as an artist. Before learning to code, my biggest struggle when creating was the frustration of not being able to make my hands do what my brain wanted them to-- that is, until I discovered web development. Suddenly, I could create beautiful things, share ideas, and convert what I imagined in my head into reality. Every day, I am adding new skills, languages, and frameworks to the list of tools that I can use to create beautiful things and express my clients' content in the most precise way possible. With a combination of to-the-pixel perfectionism and an overflow of ideas and inspirations, I am always excited to tackle the next project and see what I can create for someone else.
